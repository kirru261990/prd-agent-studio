1. Who is the HDFC PIXEL Play credit card customer? (demographics, income, behaviour)
    1. This is a mass market credit card aimed at digital savvy users who are looking for savings on each spend (rewards which provides cashback as payzapp cashpoints)
    2. Most of the users are from tier 2 & 3 cities from India, age between 25-45. Salaried and self employed make most of the users. 80% of existing to HDFC bank customers having salary more than >2L per annum. Salary between 2-20L per annum.
    3. Average credit limit given is 2L. 
    4. Most of them are spending for grocery, dining, travel (uber), quick commerce & payzapp bills payments.
    5. Men and women are almost equal in numbers.
    6. Many of them uses the rupay version for quick payments on merchant QRs. 
2. What does PIXEL Play do better than any competitor today?
    1. Choice of merchants where 5% rewards is given, 3% rewards is given and then base rewards of 1%. This is on transaction value with capping of 500 reward points for 5% and 3% category. For 1% , capping is at 2000 points. The reward categories can be changed after 90 days for a fee.
    2. 5 categories for 5% cashbacks : 
        1. Dining & Entertainment
            1. Bookmyshow
            2. Zomato
        2. Electronics
            1. Croma
            2. Reliance digital
        3. Fashion
            1. Myntra & Nykaa
        4. Travel
            1. MMT and Uber
        5. Grocery
            1. Blinkit & reliance smart
    3. 3 categories for 3% cashbacks
        1. Payzapp
        2. Flipkart
        3. Amazon
    4. DIY billing cycle date -> choose your own billing cycle
    5. Choose a card theme -> ICE, FIFA, LIT, etc.
    6. One PIXEL play can give user both VISA network card (physical) and Rupay network (digital), covers all kind of merchant payments.
    7. Instant rewards on transaction settlement instead of monthly reward cycle based on statements.
    8. Leverage HDFC acquired merchants for No cost EMIs
3. What are PIXEL's Play two biggest constraints? (tech, regulatory, bank partner)
    1. Limitations in where rewards can be redeemed. Only PZ cashpoints is an available option.
    2. HDFC Bank product & BSG coordination is slow resulting in delayed product launches. 
    3. No focus on retaining users, only acquisition is happening. 
    4. HDFC Bank is focussed on making Tachyon equivalent to VisionPlus rather than leveraging tachyon to its core.
4. What does HDFC BSG care most about when evaluating new features?
    1. Features are evaluated by HDFC Bank business product teams. 
    2. BSG cares about tech solutioning and quality of deliverable. They also look for scale and reliability.
5. What kind of features has PIXEL shipped in the last 12 months?
    1. Mostly tech based deliverables as Tachyon (PIXEL core system) is being compared with VisionPlus (bank’s core credit card management system)
    2. Rupay network was launched
    3. For acquisition, launched many features to enable partner ecosystem like bank branch, DSAs, etc.
    4. Showing & communicating fees and charges from the app.
    5. Transforming the app to web app in order to scale it on other platforms
    6. Automating credit balance refund process
    7. Very soon will launch autopay capabilities via HDFC CASA and UPI platform
